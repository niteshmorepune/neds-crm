# Client Portal guide (for clients)

NEDS gives you a secure, read-only portal to view your invoices and projects and
to raise support tickets. This guide is for **clients** — you can share it with
them.

## Getting access
1. NEDS sends you an **invitation email** with a link to set your password.
2. Click the link, choose a password, and you're in.
3. After that, log in any time at **https://crm.talktonitesh.com/portal/login**
   with your email and password.

## What you can see
- **Invoices** — all your invoices, with the amount, status (paid / partially
  paid / overdue), and a **PDF download** for your records.
- **Projects** — the status of work NEDS is doing for you.

Everything is **read-only** — the portal shows your information; it doesn't let
you change NEDS's records.

## Raising a support ticket
1. Go to **Tickets → New ticket**.
2. Enter a **subject**, choose a **priority**, and describe the issue.
3. Submit — the NEDS team is notified and an SLA timer starts.
4. You'll get **email updates** when the team replies, and you can **reply** from
   the ticket page to keep the conversation going.
5. Internal NEDS notes are never shown to you — you only see messages meant for
   you.

## Security & privacy
- You can only ever see **your own company's** information.
- Use a strong password and don't share your login.
- If you forget your password or need access for another person at your company,
  contact your NEDS account manager.
