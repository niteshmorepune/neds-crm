# Getting Started — Recording Script

**Audience:** every new hire, regardless of role. This is the first video
they watch, before their role-specific one.
**Target length:** ~6 minutes

**Before you record:** log in as any real test user. Have a task assigned to
that user that's overdue (so the red banner shows), and make sure the bell
icon has at least one unread notification to point at.

---

## Scene 1 — Intro (0:00–0:20)
**ON SCREEN:** The login page, `https://crm.talktonitesh.com`.
**SAY:** "Hey, welcome to NEDS! This video walks you through the basics of
our CRM — everyone watches this one first, then there's a shorter video
specific to your role. Let's get started."

## Scene 2 — Logging in (0:20–0:50)
**ON SCREEN:** Type in the email + temporary password you were given, click
Log in.
**SAY:** "You'll get an email and a temporary password from your admin.
Type those in here and log in. If you ever forget your password, just ask
an admin — they can reset it for you from the Users screen."

## Scene 3 — Two-factor setup, admins/managers only (0:50–1:40)
**ON SCREEN:** If recording as an admin/manager test account, show the 2FA
QR code screen. Otherwise, just describe it.
**SAY:** "If you're an admin or manager, the first time you log in you'll
be asked to set up two-factor authentication — scan this QR code with
Google Authenticator, Authy, or 1Password, type in the 6-digit code, and
save your recovery codes somewhere safe. Sales, support, and accounts don't
need this step."

## Scene 4 — Change your password (1:40–2:00)
**ON SCREEN:** Click your name (top-right) → Profile → Update Password.
**SAY:** "First thing, change your password to something only you know —
click your name top-right, Profile, Update Password."

## Scene 5 — Finding your way around (2:00–3:15)
**ON SCREEN:** Point to the sidebar, then the search bar, then the "Log a
call" button, then the bell icon.
**SAY:** "Quick tour. The left sidebar is your modules — what you see here
depends on your role, so don't worry if yours looks different from a
coworker's. Up top, the search bar finds any client, lead, deal, invoice,
ticket, or project by name or number — fastest way to jump anywhere. Next
to it, 'Log a call' quickly records a phone call you just had. And this
bell icon is your notification centre — it covers things like tasks
assigned to you, call reminders, new leads, a deal being won, a payment
recorded, or an invoice coming due. Click any notification to jump straight
to it, or Dismiss to clear it."

## Scene 6 — Overdue tasks banner (3:15–3:35)
**ON SCREEN:** The red banner at the top of the dashboard.
**SAY:** "If you ever have tasks that are overdue, you'll see this red
banner right when you log in — click 'View tasks' to go straight to your
backlog and clear it."

## Scene 7 — Attendance: check in/out AND the biometric machine (3:35–4:45)
**ON SCREEN:** The attendance widget on the dashboard, Check In / Check Out
buttons.
**SAY:** "Every day, use this attendance widget — Check In when you start,
Check Out when you finish. Now here's the important part: **also do your
fingerprint punch on the biometric machine as usual — do both, not just
one.** They work together automatically. Your CRM check-in marks you
present right away, and the biometric punch fills in or corrects the exact
time within a few minutes once it syncs. The system always keeps whichever
is earliest for check-in and latest for check-out, from either source — so
there's no conflict, and doing both is exactly what you should do every
day."

## Scene 8 — Daily report (4:45–5:15)
**ON SCREEN:** Open Daily Reports, show the form.
**SAY:** "At the end of your day, open Daily Reports and write a couple of
sentences on what you worked on. Some numbers — tasks completed, calls
made — are already filled in for you. You'll get a reminder email at 6 PM
if you forget."

## Scene 9 — Morning digest + wrap-up (5:15–6:00)
**ON SCREEN:** (Optional — show an example digest email if you have one
handy, otherwise just talk over the dashboard.)
**SAY:** "Every morning at 9 AM you'll get an email summarising your day —
overdue tasks, tasks due today, follow-ups, open tickets. No email means
your plate's clean. And when you're done for the day, click your name,
top-right, Log Out — especially on any shared device. That's the basics —
next, watch the video for your specific role."
