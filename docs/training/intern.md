# Intern — Recording Script

**Audience:** interns (watch `getting-started.md` first).
**Target length:** ~5 minutes

**Before you record:** log in as an intern-role test user. Have one project
you're a member of and a couple of assigned tasks ready to show.

---

## Scene 1 — Intro + dashboard (0:00–0:45)
**ON SCREEN:** Intern dashboard — the three cards.
**SAY:** "This one's for interns. You've got access to the parts of the
system relevant to your day-to-day — clients, the projects you're on,
your tasks, attendance, and daily reports. Your dashboard has three cards:
pending tasks, what you've completed today, and your active projects —
click any card to jump straight there."

## Scene 2 — Clients, read-only (0:45–1:15)
**ON SCREEN:** Clients list, open one client record.
**SAY:** "Under Clients you can browse the full directory — company
details, contacts, their services — but you can't create, edit, or delete
anything here. It's there so you have context, not so you manage it."

## Scene 3 — Your projects (1:15–2:15)
**ON SCREEN:** Project Updates → open a project → scroll to the notes
feed → add a note.
**SAY:** "Project Updates shows only the projects you've been added to as a
team member. You can read the details and the team notes feed, and add
your own notes and updates — your manager decides whether a given note is
shared with the client or stays internal. You can't create, edit, or
delete a project itself."

## Scene 4 — Tasks (2:15–3:00)
**ON SCREEN:** Employee Task list, change a task's status, add a comment.
**SAY:** "Employee Task, or Emptask, is your task list. Move a task through
To Do, In Progress, Done as you work on it, and add comments if useful. You
can also create your own tasks here — handy for tracking your own to-dos."

## Scene 5 — Attendance (3:00–4:00)
**ON SCREEN:** Attendance widget on the dashboard, then Attendance in the
sidebar showing history.
**SAY:** "For attendance, do both: use the Check In and Check Out buttons
on your dashboard, and also punch the biometric machine when you arrive
and leave, same as everyone else — they work together, not one instead of
the other. Under Attendance in the sidebar you can see your own history. If
you ever need a correction — you forgot to punch, or the machine was
down — ask your admin or manager, only they can edit attendance records."

## Scene 6 — Daily report and what's off-limits (4:00–5:00)
**ON SCREEN:** Daily Reports form.
**SAY:** "At the end of each day, open Daily Reports and write a quick
summary of what you worked on — some numbers are already filled in for
you. You'll get a reminder at 6 PM if you haven't submitted yet. Last
thing — a few modules aren't available to interns: leads, deals,
quotations, invoices, tickets, calling, partners, services, and the
user/menu admin pages. If something comes up that needs one of those, just
flag it to your manager or leave a note on the relevant project. That
covers everything for interns."
